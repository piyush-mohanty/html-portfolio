The Best Movies According to Piyush

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

- Studio Ghibli (2001)

This movie is a masterpiece, and my favorite anime. It has so many great themes such as family bonds, friendship, loss/grief etc and its animation style makes me feel like im watching something from another world altogether which i really enjoy.

Fight Club

- David Fincher (1999)

I love this film because it is so darkly humorous! It's also very thought provoking as well. I think the message behind Fight club was that you should never give up on yourself or others around you no matter what they do wrong in life.

Interstellar

- Christopher Nolan (2014)

Amazing story about time traveling through space with some really cool visual effects thrown into the mix too.
